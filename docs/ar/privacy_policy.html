Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Plant Reminder, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes in detail how we collect, use, store, and protect user information in accordance with applicable regulations, including the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant laws.

By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this document. If you do not agree with this policy, we recommend that you do not use the app.

2. Definitions

For the purposes of this policy, the following terms are defined:

  • Personal Data: Any information related to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • Processing: Any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, whether automated or not.
  • Data Controller: The entity (in this case, Plant Reminder) that determines the purposes and means of processing data.
  • User: Any person using the app.
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on the user’s device to enhance the experience and facilitate usage analysis of the app.

3. Data We Collect

To ensure proper functionality and continuous improvement of the app, we may collect and process the following data:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, or nickname provided during registration.
  • Plant Management Data: Information entered by the user, such as plant name, watering dates, and associated photos.
  • Authentication and Sync Data: We use Supabase for secure storage and session management, enabling cloud data synchronization.
  • Advertising Data: Information about app usage, device characteristics, IP address, and other technical data used to personalize ads via AdMob.
  • Technical Data: Device information, operating system, and other technical details automatically obtained for diagnostics and service improvement.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Information collected through cookies to enhance user experience and analyze app traffic.

4. Purpose and Use of Information

The personal data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Authentication and Registration: To enable the registration of new users and secure login for existing ones.
  • Data Management: To manage and support information related to your plants (names, watering dates, photos, etc.).
  • Service Personalization and Improvement: To optimize the user experience through analytics and statistics, tailoring app content and features.
  • Advertising: To manage and display personalized ads through AdMob in accordance with current regulations and based on usage and device characteristics.
  • Support and Communication: To contact you for technical support, important notifications, and app-related updates.

The processing of your data is carried out according to the principles of lawfulness, fairness, transparency, purpose limitation, data minimization, accuracy, storage limitation, integrity, and confidentiality.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

The collection and processing of your data is based on:

  • Consent: Your explicit acceptance when registering and using the app.
  • Performance of a Contract: The necessity to manage and deliver the plant reminder and management service.
  • Legitimate Interest: To optimize the user experience and personalize advertising, provided your fundamental rights are not overridden.

Under no circumstances will data be processed in a manner incompatible with the purposes for which it was collected.

6. Third-Party Services

Plant Reminder relies on the following external services for its operation:

  • Supabase: A storage and authentication service that ensures high security standards for managing your data.
  • AdMob: An advertising platform that uses usage and device data to serve personalized ads, in compliance with its own policies and applicable law.
  • Expo and React Native: Tools and technologies used to develop and operate the app.

7. Information Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include, but are not limited to:

  • Use of SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission.
  • Secure storage via Supabase and on local devices (e.g., using AsyncStorage in mobile apps).
  • Internal access controls and strict security policies.

However, no security system is infallible, and as such, we cannot guarantee absolute protection of information transmitted over the Internet.

8. International Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area. When this happens, we ensure that the necessary legal safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses, to ensure an adequate level of protection in line with the GDPR.

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Facilitate navigation and improve user experience.
  • Analyze traffic and app usage.
  • Personalize content and advertising (via AdMob and other services).

You can configure your browser or device to reject or delete cookies, although this may affect the functionality of certain parts of the app.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected and to comply with legal or contractual obligations. Once your data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

11. User Rights

Under the GDPR and other applicable regulations, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request information about the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request restrictions on how your data is used.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format, or request its transfer to another controller.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: myplantreminder@gmail.com. You may also file a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority.

17. Biometric Authentication

Our app offers the option of biometric authentication (such as fingerprint, Face ID, or security code) to facilitate access to your account.
