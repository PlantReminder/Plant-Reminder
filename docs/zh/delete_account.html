Account and Data Deletion

Account Deletion Process

At Plant Reminder, account deletion and all associated data is done directly from the app. This action is irreversible and, once confirmed, the following will be permanently deleted:

  • Registration data (email address, name or nickname).
  • Plant management information (names, watering dates, photos, etc.).
  • Authentication data, active sessions, and any other information stored in Supabase.

If you are unable to delete your account from the app or encounter issues with the process, you can send an email to myplantreminder@gmail.com requesting account deletion. Our team will handle your request and permanently delete all your data.

Additional Information

Plant Reminder is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with current regulations. Deleting your account will remove all associated data, but please note that some data may be retained for a limited period solely to comply with legal obligations or for audit and security purposes.

We recommend reviewing this information periodically to stay informed about any changes to the account deletion process.
